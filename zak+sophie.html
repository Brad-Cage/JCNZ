How to watch the YouTube livestream

  1. Use the direct link provided on this page to go straight to the live stream. It should open automatically in YouTube.
  2. If the stream hasn't begun yet, you'll see a countdown or placeholder. Once it goes live, the video will play automatically.
  3. For best quality, tap the gear icon and choose the highest resolution available.
  4. Rotate your device to landscape or tap the fullscreen icon for a larger view.
  5. You don't need a YouTube account to watch, but if you want to chat or interact, sign in with your Google account.
  6. You can AirPlay/Chromecast from the YouTube player to a TV if supported.
